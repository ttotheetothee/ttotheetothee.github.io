Case Study — Superstore Profitability

Client engagement simulating a retail profitability diagnostic. Analyzed 10,000+ transaction records across product categories, regions, and customer segments.

The Problem

Overall margin sitting at 12.5% with significant drag from Furniture — particularly Tables, which operated at a -8.6% margin despite consistent sales volume.

The Finding

Margin erosion was driven by an undisciplined discounting strategy. Tables were being discounted at rates that made every sale a net loss. Binders in Office Supplies showed a similar pattern at 37% average discount.

The Recommendation

Implement a discount ceiling by product category. Discontinue or reprice Tables. Reallocate commercial focus toward Technology, which drove disproportionate margin relative to revenue share.
